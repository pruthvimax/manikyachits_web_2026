About Us

Manikya Chits Private Limited

ManikyaChits Private Limited is a government-approved chit fund company with over 5+ years of trusted service in Karnataka’s financial sector. Established in 2022 in Sagara, ManikyaChits is the flagship entity of the ManikyaGroup of Companies.

With a corporate office in Sagara and a strong presence across Karnataka, the company operates in strict compliance with the Chit Funds Act, 1982 and Karnataka Chit Fund Rules.

Under the leadership of Managing Director Mr. Kanthu Alalli , ManikyaChits has evolved into a technology-driven financial institution serving lakhs of subscribers.

Our Mission

To cultivate a habit of disciplined saving among individuals and small businesses by offering accessible, transparent, and digital-first chit fund solutions — with the convenience of doorstep collection and personal trust.

Our Vision

To simplify financial support for every household and entrepreneur in Karnataka — blending tradition with digital innovation to create a seamless saving experience for all.

Management


Meet our Director's

With over 10+ years of distinguished experience in IT, administration, and project management, Mr. Kanthu Alalli stands as the driving force behind the success of ManikyaChits. Known for his strategic vision and unwavering integrity, he has led the company to become one of India’s leading chit fund institutions.

Under his dynamic leadership, ManikyaChits has embraced technology to transform customer experiences. From mobile-based investments to real-time balance tracking and secure digital transactions, Mr. Kanthu Alalli has pioneered a new era of convenience and transparency in the chit fund sector.

His deep-rooted commitment to customer satisfaction, coupled with innovative digital integration, has not only improved operational efficiency but also elevated the credibility of the chit fund model in India.
